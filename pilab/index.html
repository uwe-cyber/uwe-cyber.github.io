PiLab

PiLab is a project that evolved from the UWEcyber Teachers Workshop 2022, supported by the National Cyber Security Centre. Teachers wanted a means of teaching cyber security that did not rely on school computer networks, due to the restrictions imposed to access "hacking" materials online. Here, we present PiLab, which is a self-contained network environment where students can access various vulnerable applications, host Capture the Flag competitions, and also explore offensive and defensive security, in a safe and self-contained environment. In this way, the potential to cause lasting damage is minimised, whilst also offering a testing platform that is realistic.
